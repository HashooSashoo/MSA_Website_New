About Us

وَمَا خَلَقْتُ ٱلْجِنَّ وَٱلْإِنسَ إِلَّا لِيَعْبُدُونِ

[Surah Ad-Dhariyat (51:56)]

And I created the jinn and humans for nothing but that they may worship Me.

Started in 200X, we have grown to become one of the premiere Muslim Student Associations in the Houston area. We are an open organization to individuals from all backgrounds, united under the fold of Islam, fostering awareness about Islam in our campus, as well as beyond the horizon.

We hold a variety of events throughout the year that connect students and the general Rice community. These events range from community dinners, game nights, Quran lectures, Charity Week, and Islam Immersion Week, as well as many other service events.
