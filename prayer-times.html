Jummah Prayer

Alhamdulillah, we are grateful to have Jummah prayers for all the students here at Rice. The Jummah time and location may be in a specified place on campus. You can find this location in the main MSA GroupMe, which you can join by clicking the GroupMe icon listed in the footer list. Below are two most likely places where Jummah will be held, but be on the lookout for any last minute changes.

*Believers! When the call is made for Prayer on Friday, hasten to the remembrance of Allah and give up all trading. That is better for you if you only knew. And when the prayer is ended, disperse in the land and seek God's bounty, and remember God much, that you may prosper. And when they see merchandise or diversion they scatter off to it and leave you standing. Say, 'What is with God is better than diversion and merchandise, and God is the best of providers'* [Al-Jumu'ah 62:9-11]
